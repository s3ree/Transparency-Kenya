About Transparency Kenya

Transparency Kenya is an initiative aimed at promoting financial transparency and accountability in the Kenyan government.

Our Mission

We strive to empower Kenyan citizens by providing them with access to clear and detailed information on how public funds are being used. Our goal is to foster a culture of accountability and ensure that every Kenyan has the ability to hold their leaders responsible for their financial decisions.

Our Team

Transparency Kenya was founded by a passionate developer, data analyst, and social justice advocate committed to making a positive impact in Kenya.
